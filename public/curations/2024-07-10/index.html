Words from July 10, 2024

mellifluous
Sweet or musical; pleasant to hear.
Source: A description of a singer's voice in a novel
I came across this word while reading a novel where the author described a singer's voice as mellifluous. The word itself sounded as beautiful as its meaning, making it unforgettable.
Usage Example: Her mellifluous voice captivated everyone in the room.
petrichor
A pleasant smell that frequently accompanies the first rain after a long period of warm, dry weather.
Source: An article on weather phenomena
This word immediately brought to mind the distinctive and nostalgic scent of rain hitting dry earth, a sensory experience that many find comforting and evocative.
Usage Example: The petrichor was a welcome relief after the scorching summer heat.
sonder
The realization that each random passerby is living a life as vivid and complex as your own.
Source: The Dictionary of Obscure Sorrows
This word encapsulates a profound and humbling realization about the complexity of human lives, a concept that often leads to greater empathy and understanding.
Usage Example: Walking through the bustling city, he felt a sense of sonder, imagining the intricate stories of those around him.
