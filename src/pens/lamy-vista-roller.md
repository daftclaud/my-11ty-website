## Design and Build

The LAMY Vista is a unique variation of the iconic Safari, sharing the same ergonomic design DNA while offering a distinctive transparent aesthetic. Constructed from durable clear ABS plastic, the Vista allows users to see the inner mechanics of the pen, creating a modern and minimalist look. The pen retains the Safari's signature triangular grip section, which naturally guides the fingers into the correct writing position and ensures comfort during extended writing sessions. The distinctive wire clip, a hallmark of the Safari family, serves both as a functional pocket clip and a design statement. The Vista's transparent body gives it a contemporary edge, making it stand out from traditional opaque pens while maintaining the practical and reliable construction that has made the Safari series so popular among students, professionals, and pen enthusiasts alike.
